Warranty Policy

Warranty and Support

Valor Technology products include a standard warranty covering manufacturing defects and performance issues. The warranty is valid from the date of purchase and applies to faults arising from normal use.

  • Covered: manufacturing defects, electrical failures, and premature product failure.
  • Not covered: damage from improper installation, physical abuse, water exposure beyond rating, or power surges.
  • Keep the invoice and product details available for any warranty claim.

To start a warranty claim, email sales@valorindia.com or use the Contact page for fast support.
